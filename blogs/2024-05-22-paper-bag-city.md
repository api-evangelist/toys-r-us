Fill up the tank, let’s go for a drive! Kids can design a city with no gridlock or traffic jams—yesss! This budget-friendly DIY fuels a child’s creativity as they plan and draw out elements of their very own city. Let’s Get Started! Draw pictures on paper bags of things you might find in a city. Create businesses, apartment buildings, bridges, whatever you want your city to look like. Tip: the top of each picture should be at the top of the bag.
