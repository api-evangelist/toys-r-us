Bring the carnival to your home with LEGO minifigure ring toss. The new twist is easy to make and assemble—and it gets the whole family involved in play! So grab some minifigures and the following items to create a ring toss board everybody will love. Let’s Get Started! First, cut your dowel into six pieces using a saw. Use hot glue to secure the dowels to the foam board. Next, glue the LEGO minifigures to one end of each dowel. Lay the board down flat or lean it against the couch or stairs....
