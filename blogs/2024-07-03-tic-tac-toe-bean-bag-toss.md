Here’s an idea to toss around – if you combine two classic games, would it be twice the fun? This mash-up between a bean bag toss and tic-tac-toe answers that question with a DOUBLE YES! Gather just two items together, head outdoors and let the awesome throw-down begin. Have fun! XOXO Let’s Get Started! Use white or colored chalk to outline a large tic-tac-toe board. If playing with a friend, choose sides: Xs or Os. Playing independently is just as fun by choosing either Xs or Os for yourself....
