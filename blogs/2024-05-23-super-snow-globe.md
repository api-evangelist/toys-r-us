Do you wanna build a snowman snow globe? Whoa, that was a blizzard of Yesses! That’s good, ‘cause this craft makes it easy to do. Making snow globes are a super-fun way for parents and children to kick off the holidays. We can’t stop smiling at this idea. Avalanche of adorbs! Let’s Get Started! Use a jar big enough to fit the snowman through the top. Parent-only: use a hot glue gun to glue the bottom of the snowman to the underside of the jar’s lid.
