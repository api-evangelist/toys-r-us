Here’s a real puzzler: how can you get a lot of fun out of pieces of cardboard? You can, when you turn it into a slide puzzle! Create this diy with your kids and it not only gives them a creative outlet, but lasting fun as they square off with each other to figure out where each piece should be. A DIY slide puzzle isn’t only fun, it’s loaded with benefits, children spatial awareness, practice of fine motor and hand-eye coordination, plus their problem-solving skills get a good workout!
