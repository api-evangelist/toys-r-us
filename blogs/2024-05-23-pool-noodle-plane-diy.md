Skies are sunny and it’s a good day for flyin’ a plane! What’s that, you don’t have a plane? Well a simple pool noodle craft can fix that! Kids will have a blast making wings and rudders. They’ll learn how to weight the plane so it flies just right and take pride as it sails through the friendly skies! Let’s Get Started! Parents-only step: Cut pool noodle into 12” lengths.
